Greetings! We are the academic coaching team of KC-BANCS. Welcome to our academic coaching support app.

KC-BANCS stands for Kansas City, building an alliance for new careers in STEM and was funded by a grant from the National Science Foundation for five years from 2009-2014, and housed at the University of Missouri-Kansas City. The grant project focused on providing support for two types of non-traditional students - people with disabilities and Veterans – who are or were pursing STEM degrees. KC-BANCS partners with the Metropolitan Community College and Kansas City Kansas Community College.
