our services

1. Student Services

Students set goals for the semester or year, and check their progress toward the goal. Navigators work with Students on goal setting, resources available, and planning.Students use the app to track their progress toward the goal and the tasks related to the goal. Students use the app to set appointments with a Navigator.Students needing physical access to buildings or parking, can use the app to navigate the UMKC campus.

2. Admin Services

Registration: Navigators review the student details who are requesting services, and follow up with the requests. If the students meet the requirements of the project, the navigators will work with them on academic coaching. Appointments: Navigators use the app to schedule appointments for coaching with students. The appointments may be by phone, face-to-face, or over the internet.
